Zero Conditional

Used to express general truths, scientific facts and patterns.

Structure: If + Present Simple, Present Simple

Example: If you heat ice, it melts.

First Conditional

Used to express real future situations and their possible consequences.

Structure: If + Present Simple, will + инфинитив

Example: If it rains, I will take an umbrella.
