Game Rules

Zero Conditional

Used for general truths, scientific facts, and laws of nature.

Structure: If + Present Simple, Present Simple

Example: If you heat ice, it melts.

First Conditional

Used for real future situations and their possible consequences.

Structure: If + Present Simple, will + infinitive

Example: If it rains, I will take an umbrella.
